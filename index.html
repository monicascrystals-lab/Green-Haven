Plant care

Helpful care tips

Light

Most indoor plants prefer bright, indirect light. Rotate pots weekly for balanced growth.

Watering

Water only when the top layer of soil feels dry, and avoid letting roots sit in water.

Soil

Use airy, well-draining potting mix to keep roots healthy and prevent rot.

Humidity

Increase humidity with a pebble tray or grouping plants together in brighter rooms.

Feeding

Feed during the growing season with a balanced liquid fertiliser every few weeks.
